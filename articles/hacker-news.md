Hi HN,

I wrote this small extension to scratch my own itch. I was tired of the constant cycle of logging out/in or manually copying `localStorage` / `sessionStorage` / `cookies` when testing different user roles and states.

This tool makes it a one-click process: pick a source tab, check what you need, and it syncs to your active tab. It also has a quick-clear function.

It's a simple, no-frills productivity tool for developers. Hope you find it useful.
